Competitive exams?
Competitive exams, also known as competitive examinations or entrance exams, are standardized tests conducted to assess the knowledge, skills, and aptitude of candidates for various educational and professional purposes. These exams are used to select candidates for admission to educational institutions, recruitment for government or private sector jobs, or eligibility for scholarships and fellowships.
